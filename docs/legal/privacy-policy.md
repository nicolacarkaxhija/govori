# Privacy policy (draft)

_Operator: [name / legal entity], [contact email]. Last updated:

## The short version

Govori works without an account. If you create one, we store your email,
a display name, and your learning history — nothing else. We do not run
ads, we do not use analytics trackers, and we never sell or share data.
You can download everything we hold about you, or erase it, with one
button each.

## What we store

**Without an account** — nothing on our servers. Your learning progress
lives only in your browser's local storage on your device.

**With an account:**

| Data                              | Why                                           |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Email address                     | Sign-in and account recovery                  |
| Display name                      | Shown to you; to others if you contribute     |
| Password (hashed)                 | Authentication — we never see the original    |
| Review events (item, grade, time) | Syncing your learning progress across devices |
| Role (learner/admin)              | Access control for community review           |

Session cookies keep you signed in; they are essential, not tracking.

## Legal basis (GDPR)

Processing is based on the contract with you (providing the service you
signed up for, Art. 6(1)(b)). There is no profiling and no automated
decision-making about you.

## Your rights

- **Access / portability** — "Download my data" in the app exports your
  complete record as JSON, immediately.
- **Erasure** — "Delete my account" removes your account, sessions, and
  review history permanently and immediately. Local progress on your
  devices is yours and stays untouched.
- **Rectification, restriction, objection, complaint** — contact
  [contact email]; you may also complain to your supervisory authority.

If you contributed content that the community has built upon, the
contribution remains (that is the point of CC BY-SA), but on erasure it
is disassociated from your account.

## Where and how long

Data lives on our server in [hosting location] for as long as your
account exists. Erasure is effective immediately; server backups expire
within [backup retention] days.

## Third parties

None. No analytics, no ad networks, no data brokers. The server talks to
you and to nobody else about you.

## Children

The service stores no more data about a child than about anyone else —
but if you are under the age of digital consent in your country, ask a
parent or guardian before creating an account.

## Changes

Material changes will be announced in the app before they take effect.
The policy's history is public in the project repository.
